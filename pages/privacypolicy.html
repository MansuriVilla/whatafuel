Privacy Policy

Welcome to What A Fuel. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website or use our services. By accessing and using our website and services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from you:

  • Personal Identification Information: This includes information such as your name, email address, phone number, company details, and other personal information you provide when contacting us or using our services.
  • Transaction Information: We collect details about your transactions with us, including product details, pricing, and payment information, to facilitate and complete the brokerage services.
  • Website Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use our website, including IP addresses, browser type, device information, pages visited, and other usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: To facilitate the brokerage and mediation of petroleum transactions, communicate with you regarding your services, and fulfill our contractual obligations.
  • To Improve Our Website and Services: To better understand your needs and enhance the functionality, design, and content of our website and services.
  • To Communicate With You: We may use your contact details to respond to inquiries, send service-related information, and provide updates about new services, products, or promotions that may interest you (with your consent).
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards, as well as to protect the rights, property, or safety of What A Fuel, our clients, or others.

3. How We Protect Your Information

  • Data Security: We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This includes encryption, firewalls, and secure socket layer (SSL) technology.
  • Limited Access: Access to your personal data is restricted to employees, contractors, and service providers who need it to fulfill our business obligations. These individuals are bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business, conducting transactions, or improving our services (e.g., payment processors, marketing partners, or technical support providers). These providers are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.
  • As Required by Law: We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law, court order, or in good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or ensure the safety of others.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your personal information for any other purpose with your consent.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website. These technologies help us track your preferences, monitor website usage, and enhance site functionality.

  • Cookies: Small text files that are stored on your device to improve user experience. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.
  • Analytics: We may use third-party tools like Google Analytics to analyze website usage and improve our services. These tools may collect anonymized data regarding your interactions with the site.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You may request access to the personal information we hold about you and request corrections to any inaccuracies.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving promotional emails from us by following the "unsubscribe" link included in every email, or by contacting us directly.
  • Data Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions, such as legal or contractual requirements.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. These external sites are not governed by this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

What A Fuel does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as possible.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" at the top of the page will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
